Role: 3D Artist

Team Size: 12

Evogami is a cozy 3D platformer where you play as different forms of paper to navigate through the level. Use your frog form to pull objects, the crab's snipping ability to cut through things and transform into a ninja star to launch yourself to certain areas of the wall. Along with traversing through the level, cranes are scattered around the room for you to collect for the goal of helping the creator feel better

Moodboard

Environment Design

As one of the two 3D artists on the team, I contributed to creating a children's bedroom environment inspired by the styles of Toybox from Kingdom Hearts and It Takes Two. All assets were modeled in Maya and textured using Substance Painter. For Evogami, we focused on a simplified, stylized aesthetic where furniture and props were intentionally oversized to evoke the feeling of experiencing the world from the perspective of an origami figure navigating real-world objects.
