Soul Syphon is a single-player first-person shooter where you begin as a disembodied soul, clinging to existence. To survive, you must possess enemiesâ€”stealing their weapons, abilities, and even their life force.

Concept Art

Character and environment concepts were drawn to envision the layout of the level and theme of the game. The team was aiming for a sci-fi setting in a space laboratory. The helmet designs represent the military characters guarding the facility.

Design Overview

When designing Soul Syphon's main mechanics, we wanted to focus on the possession mechanic of jumping from body to body by"possessing a soul." This involves possessing enemies you encounter, and only by filling your possession meter. We wanted to maintain a balance between stealth and action gameplay to give the player an opportunity to play both playstyles.
